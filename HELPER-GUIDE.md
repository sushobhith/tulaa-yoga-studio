# Antara — Your Website Guide

You can change everything on your website yourself, from any phone or laptop.
No code, no files, no apps to install. You just log in, edit, and press one button.

---

## How to edit your site

1. Go to your website address and add **`/admin`** at the end.
   Example: `https://your-site.vercel.app/admin`
2. Type your **password** (the one you were given) and press **Enter**.
3. You'll see your whole site as a list of sections you can open and edit.
4. Make your changes.
5. Press **Publish changes** (top right, or the big button at the bottom).
6. Wait about **30 seconds**. Your live site updates by itself. Same web address as always.

That's the whole workflow: **open `/admin` → edit → Publish.**

Tip: save the `/admin` link as a bookmark so it's one tap away.

---

## Show or hide whole sections

The very first panel, **"Show or hide panels,"** has an on/off switch for each part of the
page — Classes, Schedule, Gallery, Testimonials, Pricing, FAQ, the Map, and more.

- Switch **off** anything you don't want to show right now. It disappears from the live site
  but your content is kept, so you can switch it back **on** any time.
- Example: turning off "Testimonials" hides that whole section until you turn it back on.

## Common changes

- **Change a class time** — open **Weekly schedule**, edit the day or the times, Publish.
- **Change a price** — open **Membership & pricing**, edit the price, Publish.
- **Add a class / plan / FAQ / testimonial** — open that section, scroll down, press the
  **"+ Add…"** button, fill in the new one.
- **Remove something** — press the small **✕** on its card.
- **Reorder** — use the **↑ / ↓** arrows on each card.
- **Change your WhatsApp number or UPI ID** — open **Contact & booking** or **UPI payments**.

## Changing the colours

Open the **Theme (colours)** panel and tap one of the palettes. The whole site changes colour
when you Publish. There are a handful of ready-made palettes so it always looks intentional, and
you can switch between them as often as you like.

## Changing photos

1. Open the section with the photo (Hero, About the teacher, or Photo gallery).
2. Press **Choose a photo** (or **+ Add photo** in the gallery) and pick an image from your
   phone or computer.
3. You'll see the new photo straight away. Press **Publish** to make it live.

**Which photos work:** JPG, PNG or WebP only. iPhone "HEIC" photos usually fail. If a photo is
rejected, open it on your phone, tap **Share**, save or send it as **JPEG**, then upload that.
Very small photos are rejected too (they look blurry). Everything else is shrunk for you
automatically, so you never need to resize. Each picker explains this, and tells you in red if a
photo can't be used.

Use a **wide** photo for the hero (the big home-page image), and **upright** photos for the
teacher and gallery for the best look.

## The WhatsApp & UPI buttons

- Every **Book / Enquire** button and the floating **Join us** button open a WhatsApp chat to
  your number with a friendly message already written. You just reply.
- On the membership cards, the **Pay via UPI** buttons open the customer's GPay / PhonePe /
  Paytm with the amount filled in, paying straight to your UPI ID.
- To hide the floating button or change your number / UPI ID, use the **Floating "Join" button**,
  **Contact & booking**, or **UPI payments** sections.

---

## A few reassurances

- You **cannot break** the design or the layout from `/admin`. You only edit words, photos,
  prices and on/off switches.
- Every time you publish, a dated backup is kept automatically, so nothing is ever truly lost.
- If a change ever looks wrong, just fix the words again and Publish. There's no limit.

That's everything. The site is yours.
